About the Event

The Wawgant Toubkal Trail (WTT) is an international mountain running event in the High Atlas Mountains. Organized by the Tougr Ikkaln Association for Mountain Sports and Local Development, it combines athletic challenge, cultural immersion, and respect for nature.

What is the Wawgant Toubkal Trail?

The WTT is more than a race ‚Äî it's a human adventure. Over three days, runners from Morocco and abroad gather in Imlil to explore trails that cross valleys, waterfalls, and summit paths.

With distances from 10 km to 42 km, every participant finds their own challenge. Experience the beauty of the High Atlas while pushing your limits in one of Morocco's most spectacular settings.

10KM Fun Trail

A scenic trail perfect for beginners and those looking to enjoy the beautiful landscapes around Imlil without extreme elevation challenges.

  • Distance: 10 km
  • Elevation Gain: +350 m
  • Difficulty: Easy
  • Start Time: 08:30
  • Cut-off Time: 3 hours
  • Aid Stations: 2 stations
  • Registration Fee: 300 MAD

21KM Goat Trail

A challenging intermediate trail that takes you through traditional Berber villages and scenic mountain paths with rewarding views.

  • Distance: 21 km
  • Elevation Gain: +1130 m
  • Difficulty: Moderate
  • Start Time: 07:00
  • Cut-off Time: 5 hours
  • Aid Stations: 3 stations
  • Registration Fee: 450 MAD

  • Elevation Gain: +2100 m
  • Difficulty: Hard
  • Start Time: 06:00
  • Cut-off Time: 8 hours
